Meet the Artist!

Hi, I'm Lillie! Right now, I've been a hobbyist digital illustrator for almost ten years now, and I run my passion project Familiarity in my sparse spare time! I'm working my way to my art becoming a full-time career for me, and I value my favorite forms of media for influencing and shaping my art into what it is today!

★Some of my favorite things are iced coffee, hot sauce, and world-building!★

Skill and Ability

I am especially fluent in Clip Studio Paint, and am growing my experience in Photoshop steadily. I use a Wacom 21UX Cintiq and it is quite possibly my most prized possession. I'm working on my comic passion project, Familiarity, so my skillset for scripting, drafting, sketching, lining, coloring, shading, and touching up comics is expanding rapidly.
