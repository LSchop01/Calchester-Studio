Meet the Artist!

Hi, I'm Sierra Elkins! Right now, I've been a hobbyist digital illustrator for almost ten years now, and I run my passion project Familiarity in my sparse spare time! I'm working my way to my art becoming a full-time career for me, and I value my favorite forms of media for influencing and shaping my art into what it is today!

Some of my most favorite things are,

  • Iced coffee
  • Tabasco sauce
  • World-building
